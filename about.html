This is our website for the module 431 - Manage orders in the IT environment independently.
We decided to make a jump 'n' run game that you can enjoy in your browser.


Story

The little figure you play is trapped in a sqaure body and walks an empty road, the only one he has ever known.
The road is an endless level and generates a new score the further you get.
The square boi wants to be a circle and walks the road to achieve his goal to buy his circle body with the score points he collected.
The only thing he didn't know was that he's trapped in the circle of life which repeats itself when he dies and steals his collected points.


How to play

Press the spacebar to jump.

Avoid the obstacles.

Try to achieve as many points as possible and reach 1st place on the scoreboard.
